Titans and Oceans (A Midnight Battleship Game)

By: GoChrisGo and John Santi

Instructions

Welcome to Titans and Oceans, our exciting ZKProof based Midnight version of the game classic, "Battleship". We hope that you will enjoy this epic, modern day adventure game with stunning visual effects and sound. Will you be a ship fleet or the Leviathan titans? The choice is yours.

Learn more about Midnight and selective-privacy ZKProof based immutable blockchain technology here:
https://docs.midnight.network/

Game Rules:
1. The game is played on a 10x10 grid.
2. The game is played between two players, one player is the ship fleet and the other player is the Leviathan titans.
3. The ship fleet player places their ships on the grid.
4. The Leviathan titans player places their titans on the grid.
5. The ship fleet player goes first and selects a grid cell to attack.
